Sustainable China Lab  (2020-2022)

As part of my postdoctoral project, “Wheels of Change: Equitable Engagement in the Global Tire Industry’s Sustainable Rubber Supply Chain Turn,” I created the Sustainable China Lab in collaboration with students. The lab was designed to support this research while also expanding students’ understanding of academic inquiry and helping them develop transferable skills for future professional and scholarly endeavors. Students contributed to key research and communications tasks tailored to their interests and goals, while learning the processes, methods, and skills that underpin academic research.

The lab accomplished a number of things, including the publication of this piece on One Green Planet entitled, "Making Rubber Sustainable, from Tree to Tire."
