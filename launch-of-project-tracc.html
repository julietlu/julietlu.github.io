Launch of project TRACC

Juliet Lu June 4, 2026

We are happy to announce the launch of the Tracing Forest Risk along Commodity Chains (TRACC) project !

Funded by the Social Sciences and Humanities Research Council (SSHRC), the project will run from 2026 to 2031, and asks how sustainable supply chain initiatives ‘see’ deforestation, what they remain blind to, and how they attribute responsibility for it?

To learn more about the project, visit TRACC.
